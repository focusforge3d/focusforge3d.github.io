Shipping Policy

Everything you need to know about how and when your custom astrophotography accessories will arrive.

Processing Time

Since every FocusForge accessory is precision 3D-printed to order, please allow 2-4 business days for production before your order is shipped. During peak astrophotography seasons, processing times may be slightly longer.

Shipping Rates & Delivery Times

We ship globally from our workshop in Portugal. Estimated delivery times after dispatch:

  • Portugal & Spain: 1-3 business days (CTT Express)
  • Europe (EU): 3-7 business days (Standard Tracked)
  • USA & Canada: 7-14 business days (International Tracked)
  • Rest of World: 10-21 business days (International Tracked)

Customs and Import Taxes

Buyers are responsible for any customs and import taxes that may apply. FocusForge is not responsible for delays due to customs processing.
