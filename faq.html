Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about our 3D-printed astrophotography gear.

What material do you use for your 3D prints?

We use premium PETG and ASA for all structural telescope components. Unlike standard PLA, these materials are highly UV-resistant and can withstand the extreme temperature fluctuations often experienced during overnight astrophotography sessions without warping or degrading.

Do you accept custom accessory requests?

Absolutely! If you have a specific telescope model that we don't currently have a Bahtinov mask or accessory for, please use our Contact Us page to send the exact diameter specifications, and we can model a custom fit for your rig.

How do I know what size Bahtinov mask to buy?

You should measure the outer diameter of your telescope's dew shield or lens hood. Our mask listings usually state the specific telescope model they fit, but the millimeter measurement is the most accurate way to ensure a perfect snap-fit.
